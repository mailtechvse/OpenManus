BRSR reporting in India is evolving and faces challenges. Companies are encouraged to address the following issues to improve their BRSR submissions:  

1. Following the prescribed BRSR format  
2. Providing clear mappings of answers to questions  
3. Disclosing detailed employee and worker data  
4. Reporting accurate and uniform energy consumption and emissions data  
5. Disclosing detailed information on renewable energy initiatives  
6. Reporting sustainability data in the prescribed units  
7. Reporting data breaches related to irresponsible practices.
